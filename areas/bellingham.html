The Bellingham Real Estate Market

Bellingham is the northernmost major city in the Puget Sound corridor, sitting between the Cascades and Puget Sound with easy access to both Canada and Seattle. Many Bellingham homeowners come to us due to relocation, retirement, or inherited properties.

The median home price in Bellingham is approximately $490,000. Our cash offers are based on comparable recent sales in your specific neighborhood — close proximity to Canada and Western Washington University drives unique demand. Our offers typically range $280,000 to $550,000 depending on location and condition.

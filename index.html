Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you close on my house?

We can close in as few as 7 days, or on your schedule. Most sellers choose 14-21 days. We work with reputable local title companies to ensure a smooth, fast closing.

Are there any fees or commissions?

Zero fees and zero commissions. We pay all closing costs. The offer you accept is the amount you receive at closing.

How do you calculate your offer?

We show you the comparable sales in your area, the estimated after-repair value (ARV), and the formula we use. Our offers are based on the 70% rule: 70% of the ARV minus estimated repair costs. We believe in complete transparency.

Do you buy houses in any condition?

Yes. We buy houses in any condition — fire damage, water damage, code violations, hoarder houses, outdated, ugly, you name it. No repairs needed on your part.

What if I owe more than the offer amount?

We can still help. We offer creative solutions including seller financing and novation agreements that may allow you to sell even if you owe more than the cash offer price. Let's talk about your options.

What areas in Washington do you serve?

We buy houses throughout Washington State including Seattle, Tacoma, Everett, Bellevue, Spokane, Olympia, Vancouver, Kent, Renton, Federal Way, and all surrounding areas.

Common questions

How fast can you close?

We usually close in about 30 days, and we can move faster if your situation is urgent. You pick the date — we work with reputable local title companies.

Are there any fees or commissions?

None. No agent commissions, no closing costs, no hidden fees. The cash offer you accept is what you receive.

Do you buy houses in any condition?

Yes — fire or water damage, foundation issues, mold, code violations, dated, or simply unwanted. You don't fix or clean a thing.

How do you decide your offer?

We look at recent comparable sales near you and the home's condition, then factor in the repairs and costs we'll take on. We're happy to show you the math.
